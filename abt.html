About Our Project

A digital education platform dedicated to empowering learners with knowledge and practical skills in Remote Sensing, GIS and satellite data analysis.

Project Overview

The ISRO Remote Sensing Education Portal is a centralized web and mobile platform designed to provide structured learning resources, tools and datasets related to Remote Sensing and Geospatial Technologies.

The portal focuses on bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation by offering hands-on exercises using real satellite data.

Purpose of This Project

To create a single platform where students and researchers can learn, practice and explore Remote Sensing concepts without relying on multiple scattered resources.

To support academic institutions by providing an online learning environment aligned with modern geospatial education needs.

Why This Project Is Required

Many students face difficulty accessing quality learning materials and real datasets for Remote Sensing.

Existing resources are scattered across multiple platforms, making learning complex and time-consuming.

There is a growing demand for skilled professionals in geospatial technologies.

How This Portal Will Help

Students

Easy learning with step-by-step courses.

Researchers

Access to datasets and tools.

Institutions

Support digital teaching methods.

Professionals

Upgrade skills and knowledge.
